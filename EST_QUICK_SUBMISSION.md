### Step 1: Type & Title

```
Article Type:   Research Article

Title:          The Collapse of Incentives: Fairness-Efficiency 
                Trade-off in Entropy-Driven Carbon Credit Allocation 
                for Distributed Waste Sorting

Running Title:  Fairness in Entropy-Driven Carbon Credit Allocation
                (≤50 字符)

Abstract:       (复制以下)

Incentive mechanism design is central to the success of distributed 
environmental management systems. Here we propose Proof of Entropy 
Reduction (PoER), a novel carbon credit allocation mechanism that 
rewards households based on their marginal contribution to information 
entropy reduction in waste sorting. Through large-scale simulations 
(500 households, 30 days), we reveal a critical incentive collapse: 
pure entropy-based incentives concentrate 97% of credits among 10% 
of early participants (Gini coefficient = 0.93), inducing utility 
starvation for the majority. We prove this Matthew Effect arises from 
the diminishing marginal entropy reduction property inherent to 
sequential Bayesian updating. To address this, we develop Federated 
PoER (F-PoER), which introduces temporal fragmentation, relative 
entropy reduction, and hybrid allocation (50% baseline guarantee + 
50% performance incentive). F-PoER reduces inequality by 71% 
(Gini: 0.93 → 0.27, p < 10⁻¹⁰⁰) while maintaining environmental 
effectiveness. Our findings expose a fundamental fairness-efficiency 
trade-off in entropy-driven distributed systems, with implications 
for blockchain carbon markets, participatory sensing, and commons 
governance.

Keywords:       carbon credit allocation; fairness-efficiency trade-off; 
                entropy-based incentives; waste sorting; mechanism design; 
                distributed systems; environmental policy
```

## 📞 遇到问题？

### 常见错误

**错误 1: 网址错误**
- ❌ 错误：https://www.silverchair.com/products/
- ✅ 正确：https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/est

**错误 2: 没有注册按钮**
- 在登录页面找 "Register Here" 链接
- 通常在登录框下方

**错误 3: ORCID 验证失败**
- 确保 ORCID 已关联邮箱
- 或先跳过，稍后在系统中添加

**错误 4: 文件上传失败**
- 检查文件大小 (PDF < 10MB)
- 检查文件格式 (PDF, DOC, PNG)
